About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at NCKU working on crustal deformation, fault slip inversion, and seismic hazard assessment in seismically active regions like the Himalayas, Taiwan, and the Philippines. I integrate GNSS and InSAR observations with Bayesian and AI-based modeling techniques to better understand interseismic processes and long-term fault behavior.

Research Interests

Geodesy, Earthquake Physics, Fault Mechanics, Machine Learning in Earth Sciences, Probabilistic Hazard Modeling
